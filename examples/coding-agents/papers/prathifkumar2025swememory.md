## Key contributions

- Direct contamination critique: models perform ~3x better at resolving issues and ~6x better at finding edited files on SWE-bench Verified than on decontaminated benchmarks given identical context
- Compares against contamination-aware sets (SWE-rebench, BeetleBox) and interprets the gap as memorization, not genuine problem-solving
- The sharpest 'leaderboard hype vs real capability' empirical paper in the package
