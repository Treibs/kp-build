Whether elaborate autonomous agent scaffolding adds value over simple fixed pipelines is unresolved: a non-agentic three-phase pipeline matched or beat complex agents at lower cost, suggesting much agent complexity may be unnecessary.

**Why it matters:** The field's investment in increasingly complex multi-agent architectures may be misdirected if a simple localize-repair-validate pipeline is a stronger cost-adjusted baseline; this reframes what 'progress' should mean.
