Reliable fault localization and repo-level navigation at scale remains hard; localization accuracy is a major bottleneck on downstream issue resolution.

**Why it matters:** Graph-guided and KG+MCTS approaches improve localization and lift downstream Pass@10, but multi-hop reasoning over large heterogeneous codebases is not solved, and localization errors cap end-to-end resolution.
