Reward design and credit assignment for RL on software engineering are immature: current rewards are rule-based similarity-to-ground-truth proxies rather than true correctness signals, and verifier quality limits inference-time scaling.

**Why it matters:** Outcome-reward RL shows promise (and surprising out-of-domain transfer), but similarity proxies and learned verifiers are imperfect; better, scalable, execution-grounded reward/verifier signals are needed to push the training frontier.
