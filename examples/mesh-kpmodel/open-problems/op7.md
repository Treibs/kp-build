Pocket-depth and hole-size legality are static, room-temperature visual checks, yet governing bodies acknowledge mesh can pass a static check and then deform under applied force; no rule or standard defines a dynamic/wet/loaded pocket-depth or hole-size test, and ISO 9554 names heat-set as the dimensional-stability mechanism without giving a netting wet-stretch acceptance threshold.

**Why it matters:** Wet/loaded mesh stretch and permanent set are the exact failure modes that let an illegal pocket pass inspection or push a legal pocket out of spec; an all-weather mesh must hold legal depth and hole size when wet and under load, a condition the rules and standards do not yet quantify.
