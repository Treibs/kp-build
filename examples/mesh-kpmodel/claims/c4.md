For a UHMWPE fiber net under marine aging, the UV environment produces the most rapid breaking-strength loss compared with salt spray or hygrothermal exposure, with salt spray reducing breaking strength from 850 N to 655 N over 60 days.

> UV environment (10 days): Breaking strength dropped dramatically, showing 'more rapid' loss than salt spray or hygrothermal treatments. Salt spray (60 days): Breaking strength decreased to 655 N from an initial 850 N.

— [[papers/zhang2022uhmwpenet]] (doi:10.3390/ma15165599)
