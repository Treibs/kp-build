ASTM D4355 determines deterioration in tensile strength of geotextiles from xenon-arc light, moisture, and heat, exposing specimens for 0/150/300/500 hours in 120-minute cycles (90 min light at 65 C, 30 min light plus water spray) and reporting percent strength retained.

> Exposure consists of 120 minute cycles as follows: 90 minutes of light only at 65 C uninsulated black panel temperature and 50% relative humidity followed by 30 minutes of light plus water spray. The average breaking strength in each direction is compared to that of the original control specimens. The percent strength retained is plotted to produce a degradation curve.
