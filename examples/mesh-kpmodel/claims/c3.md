Netting fibers in saline service are continuously attacked by UV radiation, hydrolysis, biofouling, and mechanical abrasion, which set the all-weather durability requirement.

> Fishing and aquaculture nets operate in saline environments, sometimes at variable temperatures, and are subject to biofouling, exposure to UV radiation, hydrolysis, and continuous mechanical abrasion.
