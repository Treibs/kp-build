For nylon 6 fibres, moisture reduces the Tg of the amorphous structure and has only a small influence on static tensile properties but a significant effect on long-term creep behaviour.

> Nylon fibres are particularly sensitive to moisture, which reduces the Tg of the amorphous structure. This can be predicted, and has a small influence on the static tensile properties but a significant effect on the long term behaviour.

— [[papers/Humeau2018]] (doi:10.1007/s41939-018-0036-6)
