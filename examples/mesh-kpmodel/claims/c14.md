When polyamide 6 is saturated with water at 20 C, its modulus decreases by about 80%, demonstrating the magnitude of moisture plasticization.

> For example, Silva et al. found a decrease of 80% in modulus when PA6 was saturated with water at 20 C.

— [[papers/LeGac2017]] (doi:10.1016/j.polymdegradstab.2017.02.003)
