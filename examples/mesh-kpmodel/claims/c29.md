A paraffin-wax emulsion finish can raise fabric water repellency to a maximum rating of 100, but the wax treatment increases fabric stiffness.

> Water repellency rating reached a maximum of 100 with optimal conditions at 160 C for 5 minutes ... treating the fabric with the deactivated emulsion resulted in increasing stiffness ... the emulsion can be used to acquire body to fabric.

— [[papers/AboShosha2008ParaffinWax]] (doi:10.1177/1528083707083793)
