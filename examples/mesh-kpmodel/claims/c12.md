Polyester (PET) fibers resist degradation because of their high crystallinity and hydrophobic nature, the chemistry baseline that distinguishes the ester family from more water-absorbing amide fibers.

> Polyester fibers are highly resistant to degradation due to their high crystallinity and hydrophobic nature.
