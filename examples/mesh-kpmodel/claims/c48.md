ASTM D6775 determines breaking strength and elongation of textile webbing, tape, and braided material using a split-drum clamp, applicable to materials up to 90 mm wide and 89,000 N breaking strength.

> ASTM D6775 is a test method that covers the determination of the breaking strength and elongation of textile webbing, tape and braided materials using a split-drum type specimen clamp ... limited to materials with a maximum width of 90 mm (3.5 in.) and a maximum breaking strength of no more than 89000 N (20000 lb).
