Moisture uptake in polyamide 6.6 produces anisotropic hygroscopic swelling that correlates linearly with moisture content, so wet nylon is not dimensionally stable.

> Hygroscopic swelling proved anisotropic, with expansion in thickness direction substantially exceeding lateral directions. Volumetric swelling correlated linearly with moisture content across equilibrium states.
