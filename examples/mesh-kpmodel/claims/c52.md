ASTM D3884 determines abrasion resistance of textile fabrics with a rotary-platform double-head (Taber) abrader using two wheels that rub the sample inward and outward to produce graded abrasion damage.

> technicians place the sample on a rotating platform containing two wheels, with one wheel rubbing the sample inwardly and the other rubbing it outwardly. The friction causes abrasion marks that technicians label according to their severity, ranging from no visible damage to almost complete damage.
