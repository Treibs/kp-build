A PA6 nylon knot loses about 30% of monofilament strength while a brittle PBSAT knot loses about 62%, showing the knot is a major strength penalty whose magnitude depends on fiber ductility.

> The PBSAT knot experiences a loss of strength of -62%, whereas, for PA6, the loss is -30%.

— [[papers/LeGue2024KnotBiodegradable]] (doi:10.1038/s41598-024-66474-3)
