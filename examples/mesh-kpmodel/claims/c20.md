In humid nylon 6 fibre, instantaneous creep compliance increases with both stress and relative humidity, and recovery data reveal a permanent (irreversible) elongation.

> the instantaneous compliance (g0D0), the creep rate (g2D1) ... increases with increasing stress and relative humidity. ... The recovery data show a permanent elongation

— [[papers/Humeau2018]] (doi:10.1007/s41939-018-0036-6)
