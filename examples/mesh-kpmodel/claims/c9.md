Water absorption in nylon 6 is a reversible swelling process that lowers the glass transition temperature from about 60 C (dry) to about -20 C (water-saturated), explaining the loss of stiffness when wet.

> 'lowers the glass transition temperature of the material from about 60 C for dry PA6 to about -20 C for completely water-saturated material' ... Swelling is 'reversible process'.

— [[papers/sambale2021pa6]] (doi:10.3390/polym13091480)
