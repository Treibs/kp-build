Polyamide 6 (nylon 6) absorbs substantial water, with average saturation around 8.83% in an 80 C water bath, and this water plasticizes the polymer, dropping Young's modulus from roughly 1100 MPa (dry) to roughly 330 MPa (fully saturated).

> At 80C in water bath: average saturation concentration of 8.83% ... Young's modulus reduced from ~1100 MPa (dry) to ~330 MPa (fully saturated in water) ... Water 'significantly reduces the stiffness and strength of PA6'.
