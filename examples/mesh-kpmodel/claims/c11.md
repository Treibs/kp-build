UV stabilizers such as carbon black delay but do not prevent PET ester-backbone degradation; in the geotextile study additives protected the material for 500 h before visible degradation by 1000 h.

> Commercial UV stabilizers (typically carbon black) provide limited defense - in this study, additives protected the material for 500 hours but degradation became visible at 1000 hours of accelerated testing.
