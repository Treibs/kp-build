In aquaculture netting, nylon (polyamide) has a higher initial breaking load and higher elongation than polyester, yielding a better working force under load.

> Nylon has a slightly stronger initial breaking load when compared to Polyester and its elongation is also higher thus leading to a better working force.

— [[papers/moe2007netting]] (doi:10.1016/j.aquaeng.2007.08.001)
