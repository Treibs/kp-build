ASTM D570 quantifies water absorption of plastics by 24-hour immersion at 23 +/- 1 C with weighing to the nearest 0.001 g, and notes water uptake influences mechanical and dimensional properties.

> the conditioned specimen is placed in a container filled with distilled water, and its temperature should be maintained at 23 +/- 1 C ... a specimen may be weighed to the nearest 0.001 g, then returned to the water and weighed again after 24 hours ... characterizes the dimensional stability and also influences the electrical and mechanical properties of plastics when exposed to humid or wet conditions.
