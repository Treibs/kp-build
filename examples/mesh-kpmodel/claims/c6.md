Blending UHMWPE into HDPE monofilament reduces UV-aging damage: after 600 h, the blend's carbonyl index was about 55% lower and its unsaturation index about 40% of pure HDPE, and breaking-strength decline was 43.7% (blend) versus 54.5% (pure HDPE).
