ASTM D2256 includes wet (not-immersed and immersed), oven-dried, and conditioned-air test conditions, plus straight, knotted, and looped specimen forms, enabling direct dry-vs-wet yarn strength comparison.

> Options are included for the testing of specimens in straight, knotted, and looped form, with conditions of test including conditioned air, wet (not immersed or immersed), oven-dried, and exposed to elevated or low temperature.
