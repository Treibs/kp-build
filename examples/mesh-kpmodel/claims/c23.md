Water absorption in polyamide 6 is non-Fickian (case II) because water diffusivity changes as the polymer transitions from the glassy to the rubbery state during wet ageing.

> It was shown that water absorption in PA6 cannot be described by a simple Fickian behavior due to changes in water diffusivity with water activity when the polymer goes from the glassy to the rubbery state; this behavior is known as case II diffusion.

— [[papers/Arhant2016]] (doi:10.1016/j.polymdegradstab.2016.09.001)
