The dry glass transition temperature of nylon 6 yarn is about 55 C at 0% RH (slightly above bulk PA6 ~50 C); humidity drives it down, making the amorphous phase mobile near service conditions.

> In the reference state (0%RH) the Tg value reaches 55 C on average, which is a little higher than for bulk polyamide 6 (around 50 C).

— [[papers/Humeau2018]] (doi:10.1007/s41939-018-0036-6)
