## Key contributions

- Formulates a paraffin-wax/stearic-acid/triethanolamine emulsion water repellent and optimizes cure (~160 C, 5 min) to reach a maximum water repellency rating of 100 on cotton/polyester fabric
- Documents the stiffness penalty of wax finishing: treatment increases fabric stiffness (cantilever, ASTM D1388), imparting body to the fabric
- Provides the wax-impregnation mechanism analog directly relevant to wax-coated lacrosse mesh
