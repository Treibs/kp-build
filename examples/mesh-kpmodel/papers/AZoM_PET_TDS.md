## Key contributions

- Reports PET equilibrium water absorption <0.7% and 24-hour water absorption 0.1%, with PET described as dimensionally stable with very low water absorption
- Provides the dimensional-stability contrast to nylon: PET barely absorbs water, so it does not plasticize or swell when wet
