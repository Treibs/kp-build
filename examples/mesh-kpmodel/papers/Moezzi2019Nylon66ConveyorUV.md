## Key contributions

- Characterizes how UV exposure degrades the physical, thermal, and morphological properties of industrial nylon 66 fabric (the polyamide family used in lacrosse mesh)
- Provides surface/morphology evidence of UV-induced embrittlement and property loss in heavy-duty nylon textiles
- Reinforces the need for UV stabilization (HALS/carbon black) in nylon-based outdoor netting
