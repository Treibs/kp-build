## Key contributions

- Quantifies polyamide 6 (nylon 6) equilibrium water uptake across conditions (up to ~8.83% in 80C water bath)
- Shows water plasticization drops Young's modulus from ~1100 MPa (dry) to ~330 MPa (saturated)
- Documents reversible swelling and Tg depression from ~60C (dry) to ~-20C (saturated)
