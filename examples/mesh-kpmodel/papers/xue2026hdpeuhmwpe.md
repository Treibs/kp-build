## Key contributions

- Quantifies UV-aging mechanical retention of pure HDPE vs HDPE/UHMWPE blend monofilament (600 h)
- Shows UHMWPE blending cuts carbonyl/unsaturation indices and slows breaking-strength and elongation loss
- Explains UHMWPE entangled micro-regions as oxygen/radical diffusion barrier and load-bearing skeleton
