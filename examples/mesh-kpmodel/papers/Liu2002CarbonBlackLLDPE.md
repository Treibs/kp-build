## Key contributions

- Quantifies that carbon black improves UV stability of polyolefin films, with stabilization governed by carbon-black type, particle size, concentration, and degree of dispersion
- Identifies the dual photostabilization mechanism: physical surface-area-dependent UV absorption plus photochemical (free-radical/peroxide) activity, delaying loss of ultimate elongation
- Provides the carbon-black UV-stabilizer evidence base for outdoor net/fiber pigmentation
