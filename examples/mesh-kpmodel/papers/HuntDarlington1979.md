## Key contributions

- Classic quantitative study showing nylon-6,6 creep is a strong function of moisture content (and humidity), enabling prediction of creep at constant stress/temperature/moisture
- Establishes that increasing moisture content increases the time-dependent creep deformation of nylon, the long-term driver of wet stretch
