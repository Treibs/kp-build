## Key contributions

- Measures on-loading structural strain of 8.8-27.8% that is largely permanent in Raschel-knitted UHMWPE netting
- Distinguishes largely permanent structural strain from smaller elastic creep strain (1.3-4.5%)
- Provides the permanent-set / dimensional-instability evidence directly analogous to lacrosse pocket bagging-out
