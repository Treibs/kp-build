## Key contributions

- Comprehensive review establishing why PU is the dominant textile coating: good substrate bonding, tensile/tear strength, abrasion and impact resistance, elongation, light resistance, and water/solvent resistance
- Connects PU chemistry to waterproof/breathable coated fabrics combining water repellency with moisture-vapor permeability
- Covers TPU and PU dispersion coatings as the polymer family for durable water-resistant finishes
