## Key contributions

- Compares high-performance polyethylene (HPPE/UHMWPE) against other netting twines under tensile and knot fatigue
- Shows HPPE has the best tensile-fatigue durability and least abrasion damage
- Identifies nylon monofilament as having the best knot-fatigue durability
