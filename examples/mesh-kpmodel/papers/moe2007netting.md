## Key contributions

- Directly tests tensile/breaking properties of competing netting polymers (nylon/polyamide, polyester/PES, polyethylene) as used in real net cages
- Establishes that nylon (polyamide) has higher initial breaking load and higher elongation than polyester, giving better working force under load
- Provides netting-specific (not just bulk-polymer) comparison data and a tensile test method for knotless Raschel knitted PA6 netting
