## Key contributions

- Quantifies that water diffusion into PA6 plasticizes the polymer, increasing chain mobility and producing a large decrease in both Young's modulus and yield stress while increasing elongation at break
- Cites Silva et al.'s finding of an 80% modulus decrease when PA6 is saturated with water at 20 C
- Builds a physically-based model relating water content to yield stress for lifetime prediction of PA6 in humid/marine environments when plasticization (not hydrolysis) is dominant
