## Key contributions

- Shows natural sunlight UV reduces breaking strength and elongation of PA6 net materials (mono- and multifilament)
- Demonstrates that larger yarn diameter gives higher breaking load and elongation
- Provides real-sunlight (not just lab) UV evidence for nylon net degradation
