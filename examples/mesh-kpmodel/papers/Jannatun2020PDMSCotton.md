## Key contributions

- Demonstrates a silicone (PDMS) plus crosslinked SiO2-PVA superhydrophobic coating on cotton textile achieving water contact angle >150 deg and sliding angle <5 deg via simple two-step dip-dry
- Shows the coating is durable and self-healing, addressing the central failure mode of water-repellent finishes (loss of repellency under abrasion/washing)
- Provides a model for silicone-based hydrophobic finishing of textiles relevant to all-weather net coatings
