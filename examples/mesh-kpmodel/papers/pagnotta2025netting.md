## Key contributions

- Cross-polymer review of netting materials (PA/nylon, PET/polyester, HDPE, UHMWPE) for marine/agricultural service
- Frames the trade space: HDPE is low-cost, low-density, UV/abrasion/chemical resistant and floats; UHMWPE gives extreme strength-to-weight
- Identifies operating stressors for nets: UV radiation, hydrolysis, biofouling, salinity, continuous mechanical abrasion
