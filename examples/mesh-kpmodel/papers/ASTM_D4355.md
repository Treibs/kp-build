## Key contributions

- Defines accelerated UV/moisture/heat degradation test measuring tensile-strength retention of synthetic netting/geotextiles in a xenon-arc apparatus
- Specifies exposure intervals (0, 150, 300, 500 h) with 120-min cycles (90 min light at 65 C, 30 min light+water spray) and percent-strength-retained curve
- Provides the standardized protocol for quantifying UV-durability (strength loss after sunlight) of polymer mesh fibers
