## Key contributions

- Quantifies UV-driven toughness loss in nylon 66/polyester woven fabric: breaking toughness in the nylon (weft) direction dropped ~55% after only 7 hours of UV exposure
- Demonstrates that unstabilized nylon woven textiles lose mechanical performance rapidly under UV, motivating UV stabilization for outdoor netting
- Models toughness vs exposure time and direction from load-elongation curves
