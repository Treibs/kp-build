## Key contributions

- Foundational dynamic-mechanical study establishing that absorbed water vapor acts as a plasticizer on nylon 66, increasing chain-segment mobility in the amorphous region
- Among the earliest quantitative demonstrations that water lowers the mechanical softening/transition behaviour of nylon
