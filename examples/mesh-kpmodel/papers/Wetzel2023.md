## Key contributions

- Measures the Tg of PA6.6 dropping from 58 C (dry) to -24 C (water-saturated), an over-80 K reduction attributed to water-induced plasticization
- Measures Young's modulus falling from 2.885 GPa (dry) to 0.598 GPa (saturated), a factor of ~4.8 reduction
- Documents anisotropic hygroscopic swelling scaling linearly with moisture content, establishing dimensional instability of nylon when wet
