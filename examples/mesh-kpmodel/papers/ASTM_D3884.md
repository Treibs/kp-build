## Key contributions

- Defines abrasion-resistance test of textile fabrics using a rotary-platform double-head (RPDH/Taber) abrader
- Describes the two-wheel inward/outward rubbing producing graded abrasion damage from none to near-complete
- Notes abrasion resistance depends on fiber mechanical properties, fiber/yarn dimensions, fabric construction, and finishing
