## Key contributions

- Defines single-strand tensile test for monofilament, multifilament, and spun yarns (strand-level method underlying mesh-yarn strength claims)
- Specifies measurement of breaking force and elongation plus calculation of breaking tenacity, initial modulus, chord modulus, and breaking toughness
- Provides wet (immersed/not-immersed), oven-dried, and conditioned-air test conditions plus straight, knotted, and looped specimen forms
