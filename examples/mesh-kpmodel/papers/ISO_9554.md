## Key contributions

- Specifies general characteristics of fibre ropes and their constituent materials (PA/nylon, PES/polyester, PP, PE, HMPE, aramid, LCP) used in netting/cordage
- Distinguishes heat-set (type 1) vs non-heat-set (type 2) polyamide/polyester laid ropes for dimensional stability, relevant to wet-stretch
- Provides inspection and retirement criteria, used with product-specific standards covering physical properties
