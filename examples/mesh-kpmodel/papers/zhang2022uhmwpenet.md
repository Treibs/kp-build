## Key contributions

- Quantifies breaking-strength loss of a UHMWPE fiber net under UV, salt spray, and hygrothermal marine aging
- Shows UV environment drives the most rapid breaking-strength loss of the three exposures
- Links FTIR C=O / C-O-C / C=C growth to UV-accelerated oxidative degradation of polyolefin fiber
