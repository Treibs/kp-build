## Key contributions

- Defines the pocket-depth legality test: ball top surface must not sit below the bottom edge of the sidewalls when held horizontal
- Sets crosse head width 15.24-25.40 cm (6-10 in) inside measurement, goalkeeper head up to 38.10 cm (15 in)
- Prohibits stringing/construction that withholds the ball or retards normal/free dislodgement (trick stringing)
