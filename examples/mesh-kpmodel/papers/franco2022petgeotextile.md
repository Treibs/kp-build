## Key contributions

- Quantifies PET (polyester) tensile-strength retention under accelerated UV aging (81.5% retained at 1000 h)
- Establishes UV degradation mechanism in PET: chain scission forming carboxyl groups
- Shows UV stabilizers (carbon black) delay but do not prevent ester-backbone degradation
