## Key contributions

- Shows PA6 water absorption is non-Fickian (case II) because water diffusivity changes as the polymer passes from glassy to rubbery state during wet ageing
- Couples local water content to a local Tg (via Simha-Boyer) and a state-dependent diffusivity, explaining why absorbed water both rises with humidity and self-accelerates uptake by plasticizing the matrix
