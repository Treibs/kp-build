## Key contributions

- Quantifies knot strength loss: PA6 nylon knot ~30% loss, brittle PBSAT knot ~62% loss
- Identifies the knot as the strength-limiting feature with rupture initiating at curvature peaks
- Establishes that net-scale strength cannot be predicted from monofilament strength alone
