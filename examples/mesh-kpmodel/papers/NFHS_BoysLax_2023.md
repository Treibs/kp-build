## Key contributions

- Defines the maximum pocket/net hole-or-gap size: no holes or gaps larger than 1.68 inches in diameter (an American golf ball)
- States the rationale: a stringing technique that passes a static stick check but stretches the mesh under force to entrap the ball
- Standardizes the pocket-legality spec across NFHS Girls Lacrosse and NCAA Men's Lacrosse
