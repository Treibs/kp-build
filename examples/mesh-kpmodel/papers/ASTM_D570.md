## Key contributions

- Defines the relative rate of water absorption of plastics by immersion (24 h at 23 +/- 1 C, weighing to 0.001 g)
- Quantifies percent water absorbed, the KPI metric for how much water a coated/polymer mesh takes on
- Notes ISO 62 equivalence and that water uptake influences dimensional and mechanical properties
