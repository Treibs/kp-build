## Key contributions

- Defines breaking strength and elongation test for braided/webbing materials using a split-drum clamp, applicable to braided mesh cord and sidewall string
- Bounds applicability to widths up to 90 mm and breaking strength up to 89,000 N
- Complements single-strand D2256 for the braided-construction portion of lacrosse mesh and stringing
