Rubric generation remains hard to control — rubrics often lack coverage, conflate dimensions, misalign preference direction, and contain redundant or highly correlated criteria — and rubric quality is structurally bottlenecked by query structure.

**Why it matters:** The reward signal can be no better than the rubric that defines it; incomplete or correlated criteria degrade judge accuracy and inject systematic error into every downstream RL update.
