Static human-annotated rubrics cause policy lag while dynamic rubric updates from external proprietary models are expensive, leaving an unresolved tension between rubric freshness, supervision cost, and scalability of expert rubric authoring.

**Why it matters:** Scaling rubric-based RL to many domains requires either expensive expert authoring or trustworthy automatic rubric evolution; both freshness and cost must be solved together for the paradigm to scale.
