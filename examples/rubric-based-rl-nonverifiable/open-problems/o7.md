Cross-domain transfer of rubric-trained policies is only partially demonstrated: gains are reported on held-out reasoning benchmarks but it is unclear how rubric criteria, weighting, and judges generalize across the full span of open-ended domains.

**Why it matters:** If rubric rewards must be re-authored or re-tuned per domain, the approach does not scale; demonstrating robust transfer is essential to justify rubric-based post-training as a general alignment recipe.
