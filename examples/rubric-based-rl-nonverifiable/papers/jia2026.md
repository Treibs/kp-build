## Key contributions

- Presents OpenRS, a plug-and-play rubric-based LLM-as-a-Judge framework built on Pairwise Adaptive Meta-Rubrics (PAMR) and lightweight Pointwise Verifiable Rubrics (PVRs)
- Frames reward as an explicit reasoning process under inspectable principles, treating non-verifiable alignment as a principle generalization problem rather than a learned judge function
- Instantiates adaptive rubrics on the fly from semantic differences between two candidate responses, performs criterion-wise pairwise comparisons aggregated externally (not pointwise weighted scalarization), and uses OpenRS as reward supervision in pairwise RL
