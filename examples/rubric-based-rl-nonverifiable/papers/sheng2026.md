## Key contributions

- Proposes RLCER, which augments outcome-centric RLVR by rewarding chain-of-thought with self-proposed, self-evolving rubrics requiring no human annotation
- Shows self-evolving rubrics give reliable CoT supervision even without outcome rewards, enabling RLCER to outperform outcome-centric RLVR
- Demonstrates the self-proposed rubrics also improve inference-time performance when used as in-prompt hints
