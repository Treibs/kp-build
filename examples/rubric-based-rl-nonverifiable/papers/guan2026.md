## Key contributions

- Single-policy co-evolutionary RL framework that unifies response generation and rubric generation under one parameterized policy, alternating Reasoner and Rubric Generator roles
- Eliminates reliance on static human-annotated rubrics and external proprietary rubric generators, and is compatible with expert rubric priors which it can extend with novel dimensions
- Adds a multi-level verification pipeline (meta-verifier, zero-variance pruning, Leave-One-Out peer consensus) to prevent reward hacking, yielding dense multi-objective rewards; outperforms static and external-LLM baselines across Medical, Writing, and Science domains
