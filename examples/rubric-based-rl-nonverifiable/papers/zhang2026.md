## Key contributions

- Identifies that rubric quality is structurally bottlenecked by query structure: open-ended queries yield vague rubrics and over-narrow ones yield unverifiable references
- Co-designs queries and rubrics via teacher-derived key points, contrastive rubric generation turning teacher-policy gaps into criteria, and learnability filtering of informative query-rubric pairs for GRPO
- Reports +5.5 on ArenaHard over SFT and +6.3 average transfer to held-out legal, moral, and narrative reasoning benchmarks
