Gradient regularization empirically performs better than a KL penalty across diverse LM RL experiments, avoiding overly focusing on format in rule-based math rewards and preventing hacking the judge in LLM-as-a-Judge math tasks.

> Empirically, GR performs better than a KL penalty across a diverse set of RL experiments with LMs. GR achieves a higher GPT-judged win-rate in RLHF, avoids overly focusing on the format in rule-based math rewards, and prevents hacking the judge in LLM-as-a-Judge math tasks.
