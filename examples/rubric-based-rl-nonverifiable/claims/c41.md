A correlation-aware weighting scheme is needed because naively combining rubric criteria can over-represent highly correlated criteria, motivating mechanisms that keep rubric sets informative, comprehensive, and non-redundant.

> A complementary filtering mechanism removes misaligned and redundant rubrics, and a correlation-aware weighting scheme prevents over-representing highly correlated criteria, yielding rubric sets that are informative, comprehensive, and non-redundant.

— [[papers/shen2026]] (arXiv:2602.05125)
