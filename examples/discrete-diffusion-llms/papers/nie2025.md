## Key contributions

- Introduces LLaDA, an 8B-scale masked diffusion language model trained from scratch under pre-training + SFT
- Uses a forward data masking process and a reverse generation process parameterized by a Transformer that predicts masked tokens, optimizing a likelihood lower bound
- Shows LLaDA 8B is competitive with LLaMA3 8B in in-context learning and follows instructions after SFT
- Addresses the reversal curse, surpassing GPT-4o on a reversal poem completion task, challenging the assumption that LLM capabilities require autoregressive models
