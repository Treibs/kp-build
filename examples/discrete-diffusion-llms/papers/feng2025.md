## Key contributions

- Provides a rigorous theoretical analysis showing the efficiency of Masked Diffusion Models depends heavily on the target evaluation metric
- Proves MDMs reach near-optimal perplexity in sampling steps independent of sequence length
- Proves that under sequence error rate the required sampling steps must scale linearly with sequence length, eliminating the efficiency advantage over autoregressive models
