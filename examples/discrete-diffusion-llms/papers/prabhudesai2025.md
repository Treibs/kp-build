## Key contributions

- Systematically studies masked diffusion vs AR models in data-constrained regimes (repeated passes over limited data)
- Finds diffusion models significantly outperform AR when compute is abundant but data is scarce, with lower validation loss and superior downstream performance
- Derives new scaling laws and a critical compute threshold
- Attributes diffusion's advantage to implicit data augmentation from diverse token orderings
