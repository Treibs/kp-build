## Key contributions

- Derives a simplified, Rao-Blackwellized training objective for masked diffusion that is a mixture of classical masked language modeling losses
- Provides an effective training recipe that closes much of the gap to autoregressive perplexity
- Admits efficient samplers that can generate arbitrary-length text semi-autoregressively like a traditional language model
- Achieves a new state-of-the-art among diffusion language models and approaches AR perplexity
