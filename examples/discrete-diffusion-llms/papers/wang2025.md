## Key contributions

- Discrete Diffusion Forcing (D2F): block-wise autoregressive generation enabling KV cache utilization plus inter-block parallel decoding
- First dLLM to achieve faster-than-AR inference: >2.5x speed over LLaMA3 and Qwen2.5 on GSM8K
- >50x acceleration over vanilla dLLMs (LLaDA, Dream)
