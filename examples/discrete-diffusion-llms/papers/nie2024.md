## Key contributions

- Establishes the first scaling law for masked diffusion models (MDMs), showing a scaling rate comparable to autoregressive models with a relatively small compute gap
- Trains MDMs up to 1.1B parameters; quantifies a 16x pre-training compute trade-off where MDMs match ARMs while being 1.4x faster at sampling vs KV-Cache AR
- 1.1B MDM outperforms TinyLlama-1.1B on four of eight zero-shot benchmarks and reaches competitive GSM8K math reasoning vs 7B Llama-2
- Shows a 1.1B MDM breaks the reverse curse faced by much larger ARMs (13B Llama-2, 175B GPT-3)
