## Key contributions

- Explicitly frames the KV-cache incompatibility of diffusion LMs as a core inference bottleneck arising from non-autoregressive architecture and bidirectional attention
- Proposes a delayed, conditioned KV-cache mechanism (dKV-Cache) that works even training-free on existing DLMs
- Reports 2-10x inference speedup, narrowing the gap between autoregressive and diffusion language models
