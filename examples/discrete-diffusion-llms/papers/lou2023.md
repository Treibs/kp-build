## Key contributions

- Proposes score entropy, a loss that extends score matching to discrete spaces to build discrete diffusion models (SEDD)
- First non-autoregressive language model competitive with GPT-2 on perplexity, reducing perplexity of prior diffusion paradigms by 25-75%
- Shows SEDD generates faithful text without temperature/annealing, ~6-8x better generative perplexity than un-annealed GPT-2
- Demonstrates compute/quality trade-off (similar quality with 32x fewer network evaluations) and controllable infilling beyond left-to-right prompting
