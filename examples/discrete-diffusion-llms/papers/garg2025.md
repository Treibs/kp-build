## Key contributions

- Shows MDMs effectively train to decode tokens in a random order and that this ordering has performance implications
- Proves the continuous-time variational MDM objective with multivariate noise schedules can identify and optimize a decoding order during training
- Establishes a direct correspondence between decoding order and the multivariate noise schedule
- Proves the MDM objective decomposes precisely into a weighted sum of auto-regressive losses over orders, establishing MDMs as autoregressive models with learnable orders
