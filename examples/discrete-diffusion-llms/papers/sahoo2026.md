## Key contributions

- Presents the first scaling-law study of uniform-state and interpolating discrete diffusion methods, scaling all methods to 1.7B parameters
- Shows uniform-state diffusion outperforms autoregressive and masked diffusion models on GSM8K despite worse validation perplexity
- Argues perplexity is informative within a diffusion family but misleading across families, motivating the speed-quality Pareto frontier
