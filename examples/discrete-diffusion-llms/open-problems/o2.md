Standard (bidirectional, non-autoregressive) diffusion LMs are incompatible with the KV cache that accelerates AR decoding, so naive diffusion inference is slow despite parallel decoding potential.

**Why it matters:** KV-cache incompatibility is the central reason diffusion LMs underperform AR on wall-clock latency in practice; closing this gap is required for real deployment.
