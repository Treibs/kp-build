Absorbing-state masking is one of the structured corruption transition-matrix families introduced by D3PMs, which the authors use to connect diffusion models to mask-based generative models.

> This includes corruption with transition matrices that mimic Gaussian kernels in continuous space, matrices based on nearest neighbors in embedding space, and matrices that introduce absorbing states.

— [[papers/austin2021]] (arXiv:2107.03006)
