An 8B-scale masked diffusion language model trained from scratch (LLaDA) is competitive with the autoregressive LLaMA3 8B in in-context learning and performs comparably to AR baselines, challenging the assumption that LLM capabilities require autoregression.

> Across extensive benchmarks on general tasks, math, code, and so on, LLaDA demonstrates strong scalability and performs comparably to our self-constructed ARM baselines. Remarkably, LLaDA 8B is competitive with strong LLMs like LLaMA3 8B in in-context learning, and, after SFT, exhibits impressive instruction-following abilities in case studies such as multi-turn dialogue.

— [[papers/nie2025]] (arXiv:2502.09992)
