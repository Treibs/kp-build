Discrete Diffusion Forcing equips dLLMs with block-wise autoregressive generation to enable KV cache utilization and with prediction of following tokens before prior blocks are complete for inter-block parallel decoding.

> D2F equips dLLMs with two key capabilities: (1) block-wise autoregressive generation to enable KV cache utilization (2) prediction of following tokens without requiring completion of prior blocks for inter-block parallel decoding.
