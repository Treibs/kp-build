## Key claims

- _finding_ — For a multi-scene launch video, a varied beat rhythm — quick establishing hits resolving into a held hero beat (fast-fast-SLOW-hold) — reads as more confident and intentional than giving every scene the same duration. *([judge-panel], medium)*
- _finding_ — Sequencing scenes along a rising energy arc that peaks just before the payoff makes the climax land harder than holding a constant energy level throughout. *([judge-panel], medium)*
- _finding_ — An opening scene that holds a single message with room to breathe reads as more premium and confident than one that front-loads the headline plus several supporting points in the same beat. *([judge-panel], medium)*
