As GLP-1 receptor agonist weight loss approaches magnitudes achieved with surgery, there is concern about potential adverse effects on muscle quantity, composition, and function.

> Recent studies have shown that pharmacologic weight loss with glucagon-like peptide-1 receptor agonists (GLP-1 RAs) and combination therapies is approaching magnitudes achieved with surgery. However, as more weight loss is achieved, there is concern for potential adverse effects on muscle quantity, composition, and function.
