In a large cardiovascular outcomes trial, adverse events leading to permanent discontinuation of trial product were about twice as frequent with semaglutide as with placebo (16.6% vs 8.2%).

> Adverse events leading to permanent discontinuation of the trial product occurred in 1461 patients (16.6%) in the semaglutide group and 718 patients (8.2%) in the placebo group (P<0.001).

— [[papers/lincoff2023]] (doi:10.1056/nejmoa2307563)
