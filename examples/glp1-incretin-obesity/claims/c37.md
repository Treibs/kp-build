In the STEP 1 extension, mean weight loss from week 0 to week 68 was 17.3% with semaglutide, but after treatment withdrawal participants regained 11.6 percentage points by week 120, leaving a net loss of 5.6% from baseline.

> From week 0 to week 68, mean weight loss was 17.3% (SD: 9.3%) with semaglutide and 2.0% (SD: 6.1%) with placebo. Following treatment withdrawal, semaglutide and placebo participants regained 11.6 (SD: 7.7) and 1.9 (SD: 4.8) percentage points of lost weight, respectively, by week 120, resulting in net losses of 5.6% (SD: 8.9%) and 0.1% (SD: 5.8%), respectively, from week 0 to week 120.
