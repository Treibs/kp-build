In type 2 diabetes inadequately controlled with metformin, orforglipron 12 mg and 36 mg were non-inferior and superior to oral semaglutide 7 mg and 14 mg for mean change in HbA1c from baseline to 52 weeks.

> In individuals with type 2 diabetes inadequately controlled with metformin, orforglipron 12 mg and 36 mg was non-inferior and superior to semaglutide 7 mg and 14 mg with respect to the mean change in HbA1c from baseline to 52 weeks.
