In the FLOW trial, the risk of the primary kidney-cardiovascular composite outcome was 24% lower with semaglutide than placebo (331 vs. 410 first events; hazard ratio 0.76).

> The risk of a primary-outcome event was 24% lower in the semaglutide group than in the placebo group (331 vs. 410 first events; hazard ratio, 0.76; 95% confidence interval [CI], 0.66 to 0.88; P = 0.0003).
