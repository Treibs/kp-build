In adults with overweight or obesity treated with once-weekly semaglutide 2.4 mg, nausea and diarrhea were the most common adverse events and were typically transient and mild-to-moderate, subsiding over time.

> Nausea and diarrhea were the most common adverse events with semaglutide; they were typically transient and mild-to-moderate in severity and subsided with time.
