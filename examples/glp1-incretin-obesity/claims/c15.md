Treatment discontinuation due to adverse events was higher with tirzepatide (4.3%, 7.1%, and 6.2% for the 5-, 10-, and 15-mg doses) than with placebo (2.6%).

> Adverse events caused treatment discontinuation in 4.3%, 7.1%, 6.2%, and 2.6% of participants receiving 5-mg, 10-mg, and 15-mg tirzepatide doses and placebo, respectively.
