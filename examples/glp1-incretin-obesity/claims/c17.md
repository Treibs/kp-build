In the head-to-head SURMOUNT-5 trial, tirzepatide produced greater weight loss than semaglutide 2.4 mg at week 72: -20.2% versus -13.7%.

> The least-squares mean percent change in weight at week 72 was -20.2% (95% confidence interval [CI], -21.4 to -19.1) with tirzepatide and -13.7% (95% CI, -14.9 to -12.6) with semaglutide (P<0.001).
