In the head-to-head STEP 8 trial, once-weekly semaglutide 2.4 mg produced greater mean weight loss than once-daily liraglutide 3.0 mg: -15.8% versus -6.4% (difference -9.4 percentage points).

> The mean weight change from baseline was -15.8% with semaglutide vs -6.4% with liraglutide (difference, -9.4 percentage points [95% CI, -12.0 to -6.8]; P < .001)
