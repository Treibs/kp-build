## Key contributions

- STEP 8: randomized head-to-head trial of once-weekly semaglutide 2.4 mg vs once-daily liraglutide 3.0 mg in 338 adults with overweight/obesity without diabetes over 68 weeks.
- Established semaglutide's superiority over the prior-generation GLP-1 agonist liraglutide: -15.8% vs -6.4% weight change (difference -9.4 percentage points).
- Anchors the GLP-1 efficacy hierarchy by quantifying the gap between newer and older GLP-1 receptor agonists in a direct comparison.
