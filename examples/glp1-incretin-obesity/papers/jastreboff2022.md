## Key contributions

- Pivotal phase 3 SURMOUNT-1 trial: 2539 adults with obesity (or overweight with complication, excluding diabetes) randomized 1:1:1:1 to tirzepatide 5/10/15 mg or placebo over 72 weeks.
- Established the dose-dependent magnitude of tirzepatide weight loss: -15.0% (5 mg), -19.5% (10 mg), -20.9% (15 mg) vs -3.1% placebo.
- Demonstrated the highest weight-loss efficacy of any obesity pharmacotherapy reported at the time, with a gastrointestinal-dominant, mostly mild-to-moderate safety profile.
- Reported improvements across all prespecified cardiometabolic risk-factor measures with tirzepatide, supporting cardiometabolic benefit beyond weight.
