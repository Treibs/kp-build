## Key contributions

- First phase 2 randomized trial of retatrutide, a triple agonist of the GIP, GLP-1, and glucagon receptors, in adults with obesity over 48 weeks.
- Demonstrated dose-dependent, substantial body-weight reductions, with high proportions of participants reaching ≥15% weight loss at the highest doses.
- Established gastrointestinal adverse events as the predominant, dose-dependent, mostly mild-to-moderate tolerability signal for the triple-agonist class.
