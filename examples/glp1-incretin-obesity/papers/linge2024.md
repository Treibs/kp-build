## Key contributions

- Primer addressing the open question of whether muscle changes during GLP-1 RA weight loss are adaptive, maladaptive, or enhanced.
- Argues, using MRI-based evidence, that muscle changes appear adaptive (commensurate with expected change given weight loss), while flagging older age and prefrailty as risk factors for sarcopenia.
- Calls for more objective, comprehensive assessment of muscle health (quantity, composition, function, mobility, strength) given the large future patient population.
