## Key contributions

- Comprehensive multi-author consensus review cataloguing the pleiotropic metabolic effects of GLP-1, including glucose-dependent stimulation of insulin secretion, decreased gastric emptying, and inhibition of food intake.
- Frames GLP-1 as a multifaceted hormone extending beyond its classical incretin identity to cardio- and neuroprotective, anti-inflammatory, and reward/palatability actions.
- Connects the mechanistic physiology to the rationale for biochemically modified GLP-1 receptor agonists in type 2 diabetes and obesity.
