## Key contributions

- SELECT trial: the first dedicated cardiovascular outcomes trial of a GLP-1 receptor agonist (semaglutide 2.4 mg weekly) in patients with established cardiovascular disease and overweight/obesity but WITHOUT diabetes (17,604 patients).
- Demonstrated a statistically significant reduction in three-point MACE (CV death, nonfatal MI, nonfatal stroke) with a hazard ratio of 0.80 (20% relative reduction), establishing cardiovascular benefit independent of glycemic indication.
- Quantified tolerability: more frequent permanent discontinuation due to adverse events with semaglutide (16.6%) vs placebo (8.2%) over ~40 months of follow-up.
- Provided large-scale long-term safety data.
