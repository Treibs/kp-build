## Key contributions

- Phase 3 head-to-head trial of two oral GLP-1 agents — small-molecule orforglipron versus oral peptide semaglutide — in type 2 diabetes inadequately controlled on metformin.
- Showed orforglipron 12 mg and 36 mg were non-inferior and superior to oral semaglutide 7 mg and 14 mg for HbA1c reduction at 52 weeks.
- Identified a trade-off: greater glycemic efficacy for orforglipron alongside higher rates of gastrointestinal adverse events versus oral semaglutide.
