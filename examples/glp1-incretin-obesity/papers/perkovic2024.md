## Key contributions

- FLOW trial: outcome trial of semaglutide in patients with type 2 diabetes and chronic kidney disease, with a composite kidney + cardiovascular primary endpoint.
- Showed a 24% reduction in the composite primary outcome (HR 0.76, 95% CI 0.66-0.88), extending incretin benefit to a renal-cardiovascular high-risk population.
- Reported reduction in death from cardiovascular causes as part of the secondary cardiovascular outcomes, linking GLP-1 therapy to hard CV endpoints in CKD.
