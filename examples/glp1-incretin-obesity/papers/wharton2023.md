## Key contributions

- Phase 2 randomized trial of orforglipron, an oral nonpeptide (small-molecule) GLP-1 receptor agonist, in adults with obesity.
- Showed dose-dependent weight reduction over 26 and 36 weeks versus placebo, supporting an orally administered alternative to injectable GLP-1 agonists.
- Reported a gastrointestinal-dominant adverse-event profile consistent with injectable GLP-1 receptor agonists.
