## Key contributions

- SURMOUNT-5: first large randomized head-to-head trial directly comparing maximum-tolerated-dose tirzepatide vs semaglutide 2.4 mg for obesity over 72 weeks.
- Demonstrated superior weight loss with tirzepatide: -20.2% vs -13.7% with semaglutide (P<0.001), resolving the prior reliance on cross-trial/indirect comparisons.
- Provides the definitive direct-comparison evidence quantifying the efficacy gap between the dual GIP/GLP-1 agonist and the GLP-1 receptor agonist.
- Reports gastrointestinal events as the most common adverse effects in both groups, mostly mild-to-moderate and concentrated during dose escalation.
