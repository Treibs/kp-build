## Key contributions

- Phase 3 trial of a high-dose (50 mg) oral peptide GLP-1 receptor agonist (oral semaglutide) for overweight or obesity in adults without type 2 diabetes.
- Demonstrated a superior and clinically meaningful bodyweight reduction versus placebo over 68 weeks.
- Provided evidence that an oral formulation of semaglutide can approach the weight-loss magnitude of injectable GLP-1 therapy.
