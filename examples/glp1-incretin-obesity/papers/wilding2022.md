## Key contributions

- Off-treatment extension of STEP 1 quantifying weight regain after stopping once-weekly semaglutide 2.4 mg plus lifestyle intervention in 327 participants.
- Shows participants regained two-thirds of lost weight one year after withdrawal (net loss 5.6% vs 17.3% on-treatment), directly evidencing the durability limitation of incretin pharmacotherapy.
- Cardiometabolic improvements reverted toward baseline after withdrawal, framing obesity as a chronic disease requiring ongoing treatment.
