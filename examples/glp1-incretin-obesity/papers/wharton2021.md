## Key contributions

- Pooled tolerability analysis of semaglutide 2.4 mg across the STEP 1-3 (and STEP 4) obesity trials focused specifically on gastrointestinal adverse events.
- Quantified the most common GI events (nausea, diarrhoea, vomiting) and showed the vast majority were non-serious, mild-to-moderate, and transient.
- Found weight loss was largely independent of whether patients experienced GI adverse events.
