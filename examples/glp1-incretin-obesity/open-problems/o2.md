Whether the muscle/lean-mass loss accompanying large GLP-1 RA weight reductions is adaptive or maladaptive (and in whom it becomes clinically harmful, e.g. older or prefrail patients) remains unsettled, and standardized comprehensive muscle-health assessment is lacking.

**Why it matters:** As pharmacologic weight loss approaches surgical magnitudes across a very large future patient population, undetected sarcopenia could offset metabolic benefit and harm function in vulnerable groups.
