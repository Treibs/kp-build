Long-term (multi-year) safety and tolerability of incretin agonists at obesity doses remains incompletely characterized; even in the largest outcomes trial, adverse-event-driven permanent discontinuation was roughly twice that of placebo (16.6% vs 8.2%).

**Why it matters:** Chronic indefinite dosing in a broad, often non-diabetic population makes durable tolerability and rare long-term safety signals decisive for the benefit-risk balance.
