Weight loss from incretin pharmacotherapy is not durable off-treatment: stopping semaglutide leads to regain of roughly two-thirds of lost weight within a year, with cardiometabolic gains reverting toward baseline, so it remains unresolved how to maintain benefit without indefinite continuous therapy.

**Why it matters:** Obesity is framed as a chronic disease requiring ongoing treatment; durability and exit strategies directly determine real-world effectiveness, adherence burden, and lifetime cost.
