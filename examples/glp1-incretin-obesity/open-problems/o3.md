At current net prices, the most effective incretin therapies (tirzepatide, semaglutide) are not cost-effective, and the price reductions required for equitable access (30.5% and 81.9% respectively to reach $100k/QALY) have not been achieved.

**Why it matters:** Access and cost determine whether the demonstrated population-level health benefits are actually realized equitably rather than confined to those who can pay.
