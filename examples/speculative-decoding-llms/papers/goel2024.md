## Key contributions

- Proposes a draft-model training framework (pretraining, distillation-dataset generation, finetuning with knowledge distillation) to directly align a small drafter to chat-capable target models
- Uses instruction-response pairs generated by the target model for distillation in plausible data distribution
- Introduces a Total Variation Distance++ (TVD++) loss using policy-gradient-inspired variance reduction
- Trains a 115M drafter for Llama 2 Chat 7B achieving up to 2.4x speed-up
