## Key contributions

- Frames the success of speculative decoding as relying on draft-target distribution alignment, and existing training-based alignment (EAGLE, Medusa) as incurring considerable training cost
- Proposes a training-free alignment-augmented algorithm using alignment sampling from the prefilling-phase output distribution
- Introduces a flexible conditional verification strategy with an adaptive probability threshold
- Reports +3.3 average generation score points for LLaMA3, mean acceptance length up to 2.39, and 2.23x speedup across 8 datasets
