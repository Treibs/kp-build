## Key contributions

- Identifies that draft-token acceptance rate is context-dependent, not just position-dependent as assumed by static draft trees
- Introduces a context-aware dynamic draft tree built on EAGLE's well-calibrated draft-model confidence scores that approximate acceptance rates, requiring no additional training
- Achieves 3.05x-4.26x speedup (20%-40% faster than EAGLE-1) across three LLM series and six tasks while keeping the generated-text distribution unchanged (lossless)
