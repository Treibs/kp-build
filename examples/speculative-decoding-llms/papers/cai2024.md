## Key contributions

- Adds extra decoding heads to the backbone LLM to predict multiple subsequent tokens in parallel, avoiding the need for a separate draft model
- Uses tree-based attention to construct and verify multiple candidate continuations simultaneously per decoding step
- Provides two fine-tuning recipes (Medusa-1 frozen-backbone for lossless acceleration, Medusa-2 joint fine-tuning) achieving 2.2x to 3.6x speedup; introduces a typical-acceptance scheme to boost acceptance rate while maintaining generation quality
