## Key contributions

- Systematically studies knowledge distillation for training domain-specific draft models to counter acceptance-rate drop under domain shift
- Compares white-box vs black-box and offline vs online distillation, finding offline distillation outperforms online by 11-25% and white-box surpasses black-box by 2-10%
- Shows synthetically generated alignment data reaches 80-93% of the performance of training on historical user queries
