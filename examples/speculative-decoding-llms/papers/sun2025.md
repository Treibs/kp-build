## Key contributions

- Notes that speculative decoding's efficacy in the structured, repetition-rich context of test-time scaling was largely unexplored
- Introduces the first comprehensive benchmark for evaluating speculative decoding methods on LLM test-time scaling
- Compares model-based, training-based, and n-gram-based speculative methods under consistent protocols
- Finds simple n-gram-based methods effectively capture repetitive reasoning patterns in test-time scaling
