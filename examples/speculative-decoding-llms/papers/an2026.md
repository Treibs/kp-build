## Key contributions

- Reformulates the draft-model objective to target overall acceptance length rather than per-token prediction accuracy
- Introduces Confidence-Adaptive Token (CAT) optimization that reweights tokens to align with the verification process
- Supports both target-dependent and target-independent modes in a single draft model
- Reports up to 6.94x lossless acceleration, surpassing EAGLE-3 by 1.9x on Llama3.1-8B
