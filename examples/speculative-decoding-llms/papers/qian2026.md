## Key contributions

- Frames speculative decoding's verification feedback as a draft-commits/feedback-provides/draft-adapts loop matching the online learning paradigm
- Proposes OnlineSpec, using dynamic regret minimization to link online learning performance to acceleration rate
- Develops optimistic online learning (reusing historical gradients) and online ensemble of draft models, achieving up to 24% speedup
