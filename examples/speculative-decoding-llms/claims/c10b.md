Most speculative sampling methods such as EAGLE use a static draft tree that implicitly assumes draft-token acceptance rate depends only on position, but EAGLE-2 leverages the finding that acceptance rate is also context-dependent to build a context-aware dynamic draft tree.

> Most speculative sampling methods such as EAGLE use a static draft tree, implicitly assuming that the acceptance rate of draft tokens depends only on their position. Interestingly, we found that the acceptance rate of draft tokens is also context-dependent. In this paper, building upon EAGLE, we propose EAGLE-2, which introduces a new technique of context-aware dynamic draft tree into drafting modeling.
