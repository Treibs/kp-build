EAGLE-3 replaces feature prediction with direct token prediction and multi-layer feature fusion, achieving a speedup ratio up to 6.5x with about 1.4x improvement over EAGLE-2 across five tasks on chat and reasoning models.

> In this paper, we introduce EAGLE-3, which abandons feature prediction in favor of direct token prediction and replaces reliance on top-layer features with multi-layer feature fusion via a technique named training-time test. ... evaluated on five tasks. The results show that EAGLE-3 achieves a speedup ratio up to 6.5x, with about 1.4x improvement over EAGLE-2.
