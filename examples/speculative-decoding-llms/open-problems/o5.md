Training-based draft-target alignment (EAGLE, Medusa) incurs considerable training cost, motivating training-free alignment and online/adaptive drafting whose realized speedups still trail trained drafters.

**Why it matters:** The train-once-vs-adapt cost of draft models is a deployment barrier; training-free alignment sampling and online-learning drafts trade lower setup cost for typically smaller acceleration, and the trade-off is unresolved.
