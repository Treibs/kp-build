Acceptance rates are brittle under distribution/domain shift: a generic draft model's acceptance rate drops significantly when the target is domain-specific, requiring retraining or alignment data to recover.

**Why it matters:** Train-once draft models do not transfer across domains, so each deployment may incur retraining/distillation cost; the field is exploring offline/white-box distillation and synthetic alignment data to mitigate this.
