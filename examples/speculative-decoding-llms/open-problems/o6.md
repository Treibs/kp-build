Speculative decoding's behavior in the structured, repetition-rich regimes of long-context generation and test-time-scaling/reasoning chains was largely unexplored, and simple n-gram methods can rival learned drafters there.

**Why it matters:** Reasoning and long-context workloads are an increasingly dominant inference cost; whether sophisticated learned drafters justify their cost over cheap n-gram methods in these regimes is an open empirical question now being benchmarked.
