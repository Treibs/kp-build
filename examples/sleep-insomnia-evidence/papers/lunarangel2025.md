## Key contributions

- Meta-analysis of three randomized controlled crossover trials (n=49) of blue-light blocking glasses using actigraphic sleep outcomes
- Found a non-significant reduction in sleep onset latency and a non-significant increase in total sleep time
- Concluded current RCT evidence does not support significant effects and called for larger, well-powered trials with standardized protocols
