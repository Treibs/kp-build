## Key contributions

- 12-month phase 3 RCT (SUNRISE 2; n=949) testing lemborexant, a dual orexin receptor antagonist, with a 6-month placebo-controlled period reported here
- Lemborexant 5 mg and 10 mg produced significantly greater improvements than placebo in subjective sleep onset latency, wake after sleep onset, and sleep efficiency, sustained through month 6
- Demonstrated long-term sustained efficacy on both sleep onset and sleep maintenance, with significantly more sleep-onset and sleep-maintenance responders than placebo
- Most treatment-emergent adverse events were mild/moderate with a low rate of serious events and no deaths, supporting long-term tolerability
