## Key contributions

- Meta-analysis of 20 RCTs (1162 participants) of face-to-face multimodal CBT-I versus inactive comparators in adults with chronic insomnia
- Quantifies posttreatment diary improvements: sleep onset latency ~19 min, wake after sleep onset ~26 min, sleep efficiency ~9.9 percentage points
- Documents that the multimodal CBT-I package combines cognitive therapy, stimulus control, sleep restriction, sleep hygiene, and relaxation, and that gains seemed sustained at later time points with no reported adverse outcomes
