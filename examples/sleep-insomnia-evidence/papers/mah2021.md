## Key contributions

- Systematic review and meta-analysis of only 3 RCTs (151 older adults) on oral magnesium for insomnia, using RoB 2.0 and GRADE
- Pooled estimate showed sleep onset latency reduced by ~17 min vs placebo, but total sleep time improvement was statistically insignificant
- Rated all trials at moderate-to-high risk of bias with low to very low quality of evidence
- Concluded the literature is substandard for firm recommendations, while noting magnesium is cheap and widely available
