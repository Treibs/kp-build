## Key contributions

- RCT comparing full blue-blocking glasses to partial blue-blockers on the evening melatonin profile in third-trimester pregnant women
- Found that blocking evening blue light produced an earlier onset and rise of melatonin, indicating a circadian effect
- Demonstrates that the mechanism (melatonin timing) can shift even where sleep outcomes are inconsistent
