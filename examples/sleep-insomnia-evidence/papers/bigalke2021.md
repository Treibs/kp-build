## Key contributions

- Randomized crossover trial of evening blue-light blocking glasses versus control in healthy adults
- Found a small reduction in subjective (self-reported) sleep onset but no improvement in objective measures
- Highlights a subjective-objective discordance in consumer blue-light glasses outcomes
