## Key contributions

- Systematic review and meta-analysis of light therapy across intrinsic circadian rhythm sleep disorders and neuropsychiatric illness
- Found small but significant pooled improvements in sleep continuity, self-reported sleep disturbance, and advancement of delayed sleep timing
- Reported that evening light avoidance was associated with greater increase in total sleep time, though limited by the small number of studies
