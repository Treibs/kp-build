## Key contributions

- Largest component-inclusive meta-analysis: 87 RCTs, 118 treatments, 6303 patients, including any study using at least one CBT-I component
- Reports effect sizes (Hedges g) by outcome: insomnia severity index 0.98, sleep efficiency 0.71, PSQI 0.65, WASO 0.63, SOL 0.57, smallest on total sleep time 0.16
- Finds face-to-face treatments of at least four sessions more effective than self-help or shorter face-to-face interventions, with results robust across comorbidity, age, and medication-use subgroups
