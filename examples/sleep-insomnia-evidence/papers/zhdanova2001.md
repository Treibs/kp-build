## Key contributions

- Double-blind placebo-controlled crossover RCT comparing physiologic (0.3 mg) and pharmacologic (3.0 mg) melatonin doses in adults over 50 with insomnia
- Showed a low physiologic 0.3 mg dose restored sleep efficiency and normalized plasma melatonin, whereas the 3.0 mg dose caused supraphysiologic daytime melatonin and hypothermia
- Provides direct evidence for the low-physiologic vs high-dose distinction and dosing 30 min before bedtime
- Found that subjects without insomnia showed no sleep benefit from any melatonin dose despite low baseline levels
