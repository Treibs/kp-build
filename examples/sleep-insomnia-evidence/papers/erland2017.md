## Key contributions

- Quantified melatonin in ~30 commercial supplements by UPLC, documenting actual content ranging from -83% to +478% of labelled amount
- Found lot-to-lot variability within a single product as high as 465%, uncorrelated with manufacturer or product type
- Identified serotonin (a controlled substance) in 8 of the supplements at 1-75 micrograms, raising contamination/safety concerns
- Reported that >71% of products failed to meet label within a 10% margin, underscoring poor quality control of OTC melatonin
