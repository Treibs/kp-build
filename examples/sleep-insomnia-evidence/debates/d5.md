Is mouth-taping a legitimate sleep/breathing aid or an unsafe social-media fad?

### Insufficient evidence and a real harm signal
A systematic review found only 2 of 10 small studies showed significant improvement in sleep-apnea markers while others showed none, and concluded there is a potentially serious risk of harm (including asphyxiation with nasal obstruction) for people indiscriminately practicing the trend.

*Held by:* [[papers/rhee2025]]

### Possible benefit in a narrow subgroup
Two of the included studies did show statistically significant improvement in AHI or oxygen desaturations, leaving open the possibility of a limited clinical benefit in carefully selected mouth-breathers without nasal obstruction — though further studies are required to establish it.

*Held by:* [[papers/rhee2025]]
