Lemborexant showed long-term sustained efficacy on both sleep onset and sleep maintenance with significant benefits over placebo at the end of month 6 and most time points across the 6-month period.

> Significant benefits over placebo were observed at the end of month 6, and at most time points assessed over the 6-month period, indicating long-term sustained efficacy of LEM.
