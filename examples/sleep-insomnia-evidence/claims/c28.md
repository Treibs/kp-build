All AASM pharmacologic recommendations were downgraded in quality because of the funding source for most trials and attendant publication-bias risk, the small number of eligible trials per agent, and observed heterogeneity in the data.

> Downgrading the quality of evidence for these treatments is predictable in GRADE, due to the funding source for most pharmacological clinical trials and the attendant risk of publication bias; the relatively small number of eligible trials for each individual agent; and the observed heterogeneity in the data.

— [[papers/sateia2017]] (doi:10.5664/jcsm.6470)
