For internet-delivered CBT-I, longer treatment duration and a higher degree of personal clinical support were associated with larger effect sizes.

> Moderator analyses showed that longer treatment duration and higher degree of personal clinical support were associated with larger effect sizes, and that larger study dropout in the intervention group was associated with smaller effect sizes.

— [[papers/zachariae2016]] (doi:10.1016/j.smrv.2015.10.004)
