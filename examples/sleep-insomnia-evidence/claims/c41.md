Consumer wearables are markedly better at detecting sleep than wake, with high sleep sensitivity but low wake specificity (e.g., Fitbit 0.93 sensitivity, 0.45 specificity).

> Epoch-by-epoch sensitivity (for sleep) and specificity (for wake), respectively, were as follows: Actiwatch (0.95, 0.35), Fatigue Science (0.94, 0.40), Fitbit (0.93, 0.45), Oura (0.94, 0.41), and Polar (0.96, 0.35).

— [[papers/chinoy2022]] (doi:10.2147/nss.s348795)
