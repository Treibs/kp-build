A 2025 meta-analysis of randomized crossover trials found that blue-light blocking glasses produced only a non-significant reduction in sleep onset latency.

> BBGs showed a non-significant reduction in SOL (MD = -4.86 min; 95% CI: -20.23 to 10.52; p = 0.54)
