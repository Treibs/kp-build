For acute treatment, benzodiazepines, eszopiclone, lemborexant, seltorexant, zolpidem, and zopiclone were more efficacious than placebo, with standardised mean differences ranging 0.36-0.83 (high to moderate certainty).

> In terms of acute treatment, benzodiazepines, doxylamine, eszopiclone, lemborexant, seltorexant, zolpidem, and zopiclone were more efficacious than placebo (SMD range: 0·36-0·83 [CINeMA estimates of certainty: high to moderate]).
