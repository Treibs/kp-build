A meta-analysis of light therapies for intrinsic circadian rhythm sleep disorders and neuropsychiatric illness found small significant improvements in sleep continuity, self-reported sleep disturbance, and advancement of delayed sleep timing.

> Meta-analysis showed improvements in sleep continuity (ES = -0.23, p = 0.000), self-reported sleep disturbance (ES = -0.32, p = 0.014), and advancement of delayed sleep timing (ES = -0.34, p = 0.010).

— [[papers/faulkner2019]] (doi:10.1016/j.smrv.2019.04.012)
