The mouth-taping systematic review concluded there is a potentially serious risk of harm for people indiscriminately practicing the trend and that further studies are needed to establish any clinical benefit.

> Based on the data presented by these 10 different studies, it seems that there is a potentially serious risk of harm for individuals indiscriminately practicing this trend. Further studies are required to elucidate any clinical benefit this practice may have.
