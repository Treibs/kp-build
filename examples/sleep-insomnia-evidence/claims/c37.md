The Bergen Orthosomnia Scale operationalizes orthosomnia as excessive preoccupation with sleep, captured by two factors ('interference' and 'rigidity'), each comprising six items.

> This study aimed to extend the knowledge about orthosomnia, that is, excessive preoccupation with sleep, by developing a scale for its assessment.
