Across four consumer wearables tested at home, sleep-stage tracking showed high variability, so the devices in their current form are best used for sleep-wake outcomes rather than sleep stages.

> High variability in sleep stage-tracking performance suggests that these devices, in their current form, are still best utilized for tracking sleep-wake outcomes and not sleep stages.

— [[papers/chinoy2022]] (doi:10.2147/nss.s348795)
