In phase 3 trials, daridorexant 50 mg significantly reduced wake time after sleep onset versus placebo at month 1 (LSM difference -22.8 min) and month 3 (-18.3 min).

> WASO and LPS were significantly reduced among participants in the daridorexant 50 mg group compared with participants in the placebo group at month 1 (least squares mean [LSM] difference -22·8 min [95% CI -28·0 to -17·6], p<0·0001 for WASO; -11·4 min [-16·0 to -6·7], p<0·0001 for LPS) and month 3 (-18·3 min [-23·9 to -12·7], p<0·0001 for WASO; -11·7 min [-16·3 to -7·0], p<0·0001 for LPS)
