Individual variability and equity gaps in wearable sensor performance are unresolved.

**Why it matters:** Person-specific factors such as skin color and obesity can degrade sensor accuracy, risking augmentation of existing healthcare disparities, and translation of device performance to people with comorbidities is unclear.
