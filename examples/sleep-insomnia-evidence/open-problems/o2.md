Long-term and head-to-head efficacy/safety data for newer insomnia pharmacotherapies (including dual orexin antagonists) are incomplete.

**Why it matters:** Network meta-analysis flags that several licensed drugs lack long-term safety information and that for agents such as daridorexant and suvorexant the long-term/tolerability picture is poor or absent; without it, ranking and durable prescribing decisions stay uncertain.

**Flagged by:** [[papers/decrescenzo2022]] (doi:10.1016/s0140-6736(22)00878-9), [[papers/sateia2017]] (doi:10.5664/jcsm.6470)
