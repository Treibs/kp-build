CBT-I is first-line and efficacious, but making it available and accessible at population scale remains unsolved; therapist scarcity drives patients toward medication instead.

**Why it matters:** Guidelines (ACP) make CBT-I the strong first recommendation, yet inadequate resources mean medications are used more frequently worldwide; digital/internet CBT-I is a proposed but incomplete fix whose effects depend on personal clinical support and are eroded by dropout.
