Write a Manim CE (v0.20.1) scene. A pixel-art character sprite is in the working directory as
`sprite.png` (16×16). The character, 1.5 units tall with crisp pixels, runs one full lap
around a rectangular track — the perimeter of a 10×5 rectangle centered on screen (drawn as a
visible outline) — at constant speed, taking about 6 seconds, ending where it started. While
running the character must face its direction of travel: it looks right on the top and bottom
edges when moving right, and is mirrored horizontally whenever it moves left.
