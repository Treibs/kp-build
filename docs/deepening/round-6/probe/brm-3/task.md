A pass/fail examiner: candidates submit a numeric score (0–100; anything higher is invalid
and the submission is rejected outright) with an exam fee. The grader (capability) grades
each submission: at or above the cutoff, a `Certificate` object is minted to the candidate
and the fee is banked; below the cutoff, the candidate gets the fee back minus a fixed
grading charge (which is banked), and nothing is minted. Views: certificates issued and the
bank total.
