A sprite-sheet walk cycle: the working directory contains `sprite_sheet.png` — a 64×16 sheet
holding four 16×16 walk frames side by side (order: step, stand, step-mirrored, stand). Cut
the four frames OUT OF THE SHEET (no per-frame files exist) and play the character walking
in place for 4 seconds, cycling the cut frames every 0.25 seconds, 2 units tall, crisp.
