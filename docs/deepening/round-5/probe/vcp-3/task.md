A barber shop walk-in queue: customers walk in and present the haircut fee in SUI. If the
barber (capability holder marks themselves in or out per epoch) is IN, the haircut is logged
to the customer's visit count and the payment joins the shop's pool; if the barber is OUT
that epoch, the walk-in is turned away and the presented payment is returned whole. The owner
sweeps the pool. Views: a customer's visit count and whether the barber is in.
