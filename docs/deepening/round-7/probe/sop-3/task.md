A night pharmacy roster: pharmacists sign up as available by paying a refundable retainer in
SUI, held by the roster; the duty officer (capability) appoints one of them on-call — the
slot may be empty between appointments. An emergency dispatch names the current on-call
pharmacist in a `Dispatched` event and vacates the slot; the retainer returns to a
pharmacist when they leave the roster unappointed. Views: the on-call pharmacist if one is
appointed, and the roster's held retainer total.
