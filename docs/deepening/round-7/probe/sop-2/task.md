An aquarium's tank hall: numbered tanks each optionally hold an exhibit (id and label). The
warden (capability) stocks an exhibit into an empty tank, retires one to storage (the tank
goes empty), or moves one between tanks; every stocking is stamped with the time from the
on-chain clock and announced in a `Stocked` event. Views: the exhibit label in a tank if
any, and how many tanks are currently stocked.
