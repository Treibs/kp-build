A marina waitlist: berth applicants join an ordered waitlist recording who applied and the
boat length they need. When a berth opens (the harbormaster, capability, frees it with its
length), the first waitlist entry whose boat fits is assigned and pays the posted mooring
deposit in SUI, held by the marina per berth. Views: the next waitlist entry (applicant and
requested length) if any, and a berth's held deposit amount.
